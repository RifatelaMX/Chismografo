## Overview

Modern minimalist high-contrast aesthetic with sharp lines, dark mode background, neon accents, and responsive 8px baseline grids, conforming to WCAG 2.1 AA contrast principles.

## Style Foundations

- **Visual style:** modern, high-contrast, cyberpunk-minimalist
- **Typography scale:** desktop-first expressive scale
- **Typography fonts:** primary=Inter, display=Inter, mono=JetBrains Mono
- **Typography weights:** 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
- **Color palette:** primary, neutral, success, warning, danger
- **Spacing scale:** 8pt baseline grid (8px, 16px, 24px)

## Colors

- **Primary (#ADFF00):** Lime Green accent, used for major highlights, metrics values, and primary borders.
- **Secondary (#25D366):** WhatsApp Green accent, used for success statuses, active badges, and button gradient anchors.
- **Success (#25D366):** WhatsApp Green, indicating positive audit results and online status.
- **Warning (#F59E0B):** Orange, indicating intermediate performance levels or warnings.
- **Danger (#EF4444):** Red, indicating critical errors, low performance scores, or offline status.
- **Background (#000000):** Pure Black 60% domain canvas with sutil dot grid.
- **Surface (#121212):** Matte Dark Gray 30% container surface for glassmorphic cards.
- **Text (#FFFFFF):** High Contrast White for ultimate reading clarity on dark backgrounds.
- **Text-Secondary (#A3A3A3):** Light gray for secondary text and descriptions.
- **Text-Muted (#666666):** Medium gray for timestamps, metadata, and borders.
